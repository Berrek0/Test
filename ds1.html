DARK SOULS - SL1 Guides and Resources

Rules/Guidelines:

For SL1 Champion we require all bosses to be defeated with screenshots/videos of the following:

  • Stray Demon's empty arena
  • Taurus Demon's empty arena
  • Gargoyles empty arena
  • Capra Demon's Key to the Depths or Capra Demon's empty arena
  • Gaping Dragon's Key to Blighttown or Gaping Dragon's empty arena
  • Demon's Catalyst or Demon Firesage's empty arena
  • Centipede Demon's empty arena
  • Calamity Ring or Kalameet's empty arena
  • All boss souls, an item made from them (if no longer have them) or their bonfires/empty arenas
  • Stats

Reinforced Club is your main weapon for most of the game - you'll want to eventually have a Chaos Reinforced Club (and possibly a Lightning Reinforced Club if you don't want to or can't use Blacksmith Giant Hammer). Fire for early game until you can upgrade to Chaos is fine. Blacksmith Giant Hammer is the other best weapon of choice, you alternate between them based on boss resistances.

DARK SOULS - SL1 Additional Notes

As long as the run allows it, cheesing Bed of Chaos and Ceaseless Discharge is fine.

DARK SOULS - Acceptable SL1 Legend Criteria

  • SL1 NG+6
  • SL1 No Rolling/Blocking/Parrying run
  • SL1 +0 Weapons, +0 Armor, Normal Upgrade Path, No Bleed, No Toxic, No Poison run
  • SL1 & All Bosses Speedrun under ??? hours ??? minutes - Mandatory: Includes Artorias of the Abyss
  • SL1 Perma-Death run - Record all of the run (Seath's mandatory death does not count if you don't want to attempt the skip)
  • SL1 Not Hit run & Any% - Record all of the run (Seath's mandatory death does not count if you don't want to attempt the skip)
  • SL1 Zero Weapon Durability - e.g. the weapon is broken, also called "Broken Weapon run"
  • SL1 Blindfolded run - Not allowed to see the screen, needs a clear webcam stream to prove
  • SL1 Fist only - It's recommended Four Kings should be beaten with a different Legend challenge. Red Tear Stone Ring and Power Within are allowed versus Four Kings
  • SL1 Caestus only - Red Tear Stone Ring and Power Within are allowed versus Four Kings
  • SL1 Broken Straight Sword only - Red Tear Stone Ring and Power Within are allowed versus Four Kings
  • SL1 Straight Sword Hilt only - Red Tear Stone Ring and Power Within are allowed versus Four Kings

DARK SOULS - SL1 Legend Banned Equipment

  • Usage of Red Tear Stone Ring on base NG runs unless specified - It's too strong
  • Usage of offensive Pyromancy spells unless specified - Power Within, Great Combustion, Black Flame, Fireorb and Great Chaos Fireball are too strong
