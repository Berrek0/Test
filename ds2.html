DARK SOULS II - SL1 Guides and Resources

Rules/Guidelines:

For SL1 Champion we require all bosses to be defeated with screenshots/videos of the following:

  • Aldia's defeated dialogue, the visible choice to pick 'Leave Throne' or something from the Leave Throne ending
  • Ancient Dragon's empty arena
  • Flower Skirt or Graverobber, Varg and Cerah's empty arena
  • Pharros Mask, Aged Smelter Sword (Blue Smelter Demon weapon) or Blue Smelter Demon's empty arena
  • All boss souls*, an item made from them (if no longer have them) or their bonfires/empty arenas** - * Includes Vendrick's
  • Stats

Route by Xerieh:

  1. Use Dagger until you can use Mace, use Mace until you can use Rapier with Cale's Helm and Work Hook.
  2. Immediately go to the start of the Iron King DLC so you can get Dexterity Ring, you don't want to lose AGI if you are rolling.
  3. You can use Homunculus Mace with Strength Ring (or Mace if you don't have Strength Ring) for Dragonrider, Twin Dragonriders, Smelter Demon, Mirror Knight, Ruin Sentinels and Skeleton Lords; for DLC bosses, Fume Knight, Blue Smelter, and Velstadt/skeletons for Elana.
    All else should be fought with Rapier.

DARK SOULS II - Acceptable SL1 Legend Criteria
Notes: 'CoC' means you have to be in the Company of Champions covenant. Please ignore CoC requirements for Darklurker as you need to change covenant to access it.

  • SL1 NG+7
  • SL1 & CoC No Rolling/Blocking/Parrying/Red Tearstone Ring* run - *Red Tearstone Ring is banned on base NG runs as it makes this run trivial
  • SL1 & CoC +0 Weapons, +0 Armour, Normal Upgrade Path, No Bleed, No Toxic, No Poison run
  • SL1 All Bosses Speedrun under ??? hours ??? minutes - Mandatory: Includes The Lost Crowns trilogy
  • SL1 & CoC Perma-Death run - Record all of the run
  • SL1 & CoC Not Hit run & Any% - Record all of the run
  • SL1 & CoC Zero Weapon Durability - e.g. the weapon is broken, also called “Broken Weapon run”
  • SL1 Blindfolded run - Not allowed to see the screen, needs a clear webcam stream to prove
  • SL1 & CoC Fist only - Vanquisher's Seal is NOT allowed, please show your rings / equipment screen at the end of the fight
  • SL1 & CoC Broken Ladle only
  • SL1 & CoC Broken Thief Sword only
  • SL1 & CoC Broken Straight Sword only
