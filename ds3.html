DARK SOULS III - Acceptable SL1 Legend Criteria

  • SL1 NG+7
  • SL1 No Rolling/Blocking/Parrying run
  • SL1 +0 Weapons, No infusions, No Bleed, No Toxic, No Poison, No Frost, No Dark Hand run
  • SL1 Perma-Death run - Record all of the run, Tears of Denial is allowed for this run
  • SL1 Not Hit run & Any% - Record all of the run
  • SL1 NG+ Zero Weapon Durability, No Bleed, No Toxic, No Poison, No Frost, No Buffs, No Dark Hand run - e.g. the weapon is broken, also called “Broken Weapon run”
  • SL1 NG+ No Rings, Infusions, buffs - Includes buffs from weapon Skills
  • SL1 All Bosses Speedrun under 2 hours 10 minutes - Mandatory: Includes both Ashes of Ariandel and The Ringed City
  • SL-80 NG+ - This means Soul Level minus 80, obtained by using exploits or cheats so you have only 1 in every stat
  • SL1 Blindfolded run - Not allowed to see the screen, needs a clear webcam stream to prove
  • SL1 Armour of thorns only - It's recommended Yhorm, SoC, Halflight, Gravetender, Deacons and Wolnir should be beaten with a different Legend challenge.
  • SL1 Fists only - It's recommended SoC, Wolnir, Halflight, Gravetender and Deacons should be beaten with a different Legend challenge
  • SL1 Torch only - It's recommended Iudex, Deacons and Yhorm should be beaten with a different Legend challenge
  • SL1 Soldering Iron only - It's recommended Yhorm should be beaten with a different Legend challenge

DARK SOULS III - SL1 Legend Banned Equipment

  • Usage of Tears of Denial - Promotes error-prone combat, avoiding death via equipment is already simple. Is okay for Perma-Death runs.
  • Usage of Morion Blade - It's too strong. However this is allowed on Perma-Death runs
  • Usage of Storm Ruler against Yhorm - It's too strong, this also means Siegward versus Yhorm is unacceptable.
  • Usage of Storyteller's Staff against bosses - The weapon Skill makes boss fights way too passive with little risk

DARK SOULS III - Runs aren't considered challenging enough for SL1 Legend

  • Any other class that isn't Deprived - Not SL1
  • SL1 First Person - It's just as playable
  • SL1 no armour run - Since you get 1 or 2 shot by most things anyway this alone wouldn't really be a challenge
  • SL1 no healing run - Again, since you get 1 or 2 shot by most things it really doesn't matter if you're healing or not
  • SL1 [insert weapon] run - If the weapon is a very bad weapon it could be considered for Legend Run. If the weapon is something decent like the ‘Astora Straight Sword'; then no it shouldn't count for Legend run
  • SL1 NG Dark hand run - Not weak enough
  • SL1 Keyboard and Mouse run - Since some people prefer to play on Keyboard and Mouse I don't think this should count
  • SL1 No Weapon buffs - Since most of the damage comes from Rings and not Resins/Bundles/Spells especially against the DLC bosses, simply not using Weapon buffs isn't enough for Legend runs (and besides, you can just bleed bosses with a blood infused bandit knife and kill them really fast)

DARK SOULS III - SL1 Weapon & Equipment Information

Usable weapons data by Decc:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/15PBJkSpoXs9gWpngOPUP-YNrfps0CWFb45vuHCorx-s/edit#gid=0

Recommended:

  • Raw Broadsword & Fire Broadsword:
    Like a faster Millwood Battle Axe but with less damage. Good for general damage on fast bosses and Raw allows poison application. Can be used with Lothric War Banner

  • Raw Dragonslayer's Axe:
    Otherwise known as DSA. Godlike weapon, you use this on almost everything

  • Sharp Flamberge:
    High stagger potential, good damage, bleed.

  • Blood Bandit's Knife:
    Bleed is good.

  • Heavy Millwood Battle Axe:
    Basically DSA but with phys instead of split, good for bosses resistant to lightning but not difficult to attack, can use with Lothric War Banner.

  • Profaned Greatsword:
    Basically for charged R2 cheese on some bosses, notably Halflight.

  • Crossbows:
    Mostly for punishing, cheezy tactic PagChomp.

  • Storyteller's Staff:
    Poison that scales with max health, tons of damage. :pray: :pray: :pray:

  • Poison Rotten Ghru Dagger:
    Procs poison hellishly fast.

  • Deep/Fire/Sharp Dagger:
    Good crit.
